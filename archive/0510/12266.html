Kurzweil assumes a gradually quickening progression in achieving singularity-ish technologies. I think that there are a few technologies, AGI and MNT in particular, where progress, regression, extinction, or even a permanent negative human quality-of-living are possible. The occurance of these events are discreet levers in history and will not be subject to any smooth technological curve.
