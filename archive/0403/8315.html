The Singularity Institute's weekly volunteer meeting is this Sunday at 7 PM
EST (GMT-5). The IRC server is sl4.org, port 6667; chat room #siaiv.

This meeting will be on ways to focus marketing and donor raising efforts
and on ideas for an "Action" section at SIAI's site.

To participate through Java applet, go to:
http://sl4.org/chat/ (use #siaiv for the "Room to Enter" field)

To clear any confusion about timezones, go to:
http://www.time.gov/timezone.cgi?Eastern/d/-5
