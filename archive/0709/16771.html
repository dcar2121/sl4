Sorry folks, this was meant as an ESSAY, I forgot to put that in the subject.

Eliezer Yudkowsky, I was wondering about your experiences regarding
procrastination. I find your writings greatly inspiring and I noticed
that you amassed and enormous amount of knowledge through independent
studies outside of academia. I wonder how you managed to maintain the
high level of discipline and structure required for this. Did or do
you have a mentor?

Thanks, Roland Anderson.
