does..?), but I just thought I'd point out that he has read Kurzweil's "Age
of Spiritual Machines".
    In case you didn't know, here's what he had to say: "Ray's technology
and ideas have truly been among the sunshines of my life. This book is a
wonderful riff on the next century from a keen seer, a great inventor, and a
good friend."
