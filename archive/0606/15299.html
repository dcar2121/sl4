At the AGIRI conference last month, Steve Grand told me about a new
website someone he knows just put up: www.machineslikeus.com
It's about AI, ALife, evolution, & philosophy, and may be a better
venue for philosophical discussions than SL4. The forums are still
pretty sparse, but every day the owner puts up links to several
interesting high-SL news articles.
