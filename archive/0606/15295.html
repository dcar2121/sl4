Locus Awards Winners

Winners of this year's Locus Awards, voted by readers of Locus
Magazine in the annual Locus Poll, were at the Science Fiction Museum
and Hall of Fame in Seattle.

Best Science Fiction Novel

Accelerando, Charles Stross (Ace; Orbit)
