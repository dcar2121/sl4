Yep, that's called Crocker's Rules; it's in the
wiki, I'm sure. The problem with pure ad hominem
is that it lowers the signal-to-noise ratio and
cries out for killthread. Put it another way:
suppose this were the interoffice E-mail at NASA
dedicated to building that rocketship to save the
human race before Worlds Collide. How much of
that interoffice E-mail should be wasted on flame
wars or irrelevant gab about old technologies not
central to building the rocket? Ideally, none.
Hence killthreads.
