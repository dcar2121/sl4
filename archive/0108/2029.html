Well guys, I guess we all agree. Once we get to clever-human-level
intelligence in an AI, the singularity will be *relatively* quick.

Whether "quick" will be nanoseconds or years, we don't all agree on -- and
we aren't likely to agree until we have more advanced AI's to play around
with, to gather better real data to ground our intuitions.

This kind of chat is fun, but the real trick is getting to
clever-human-level intelligence, huh?

On that note, which I've repeated rather often I admit, I'll stop reading
e-mails and get back to work ;>
