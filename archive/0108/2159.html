Although it's probably a bit late in the game to be saying this, it's
worth noting that at least two SL4 members (including me), and possibly
more, will be at Dragoncon in Atlanta, Aug 31 through Sep 3. If anyone
else is heading there and is interested in a rendezvous, drop me a note
and I'll reply with my cellphone number.
