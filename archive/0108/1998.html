>
> So would you say your gut feel is that doubling the resources would only
> lead to a doubling or less in raw intelligence? Just curious...
> --

My gut is that the dependence of intelligence on resources will be highly
nonlinear.

there's a phase transition when the resources are enough to allow practical
schema learning on the system's own cognition control schema

there may be other phase transitions as well

so a doubling of resources will lead to MORE than a doubling in raw
intelligence sometimes, and LESS than a doubling sometimes, depending on the
precise resource amount involved. This is the subtletly and the
frustratingness of nonlinearity.
