The common figure referred to in semiconductor
process descriptions is the 'DRAM half pitch'
which is half the center-to-center spacing of
parallel circuit lines in a DRAM chip. When
chip makers refer to the 130 nm feature size
they are introducing this year, that is the
particular feature they are referring to. For
an in-depth discussion of semiconductor technology
projections, see the International Technology
Roadmap for Semiconductors at:

http://public.itrs.net/Files/2001ITRS/Home.htm
