> But does it make sense to make bias into the highest priority?
> Isn't it more important to find a good design for artificial
> intelligence than to identify the problems in something quite

I think you're arguing against a straw man. I don't think anyone is
seriously making the study of biases the highest priority of the
community. Yes, it is more important to find a good design for
artificial intelligence, and part of that is understanding biases,

you allocate an appropriate amount of time to correcting our error.
