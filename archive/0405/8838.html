>Why do you believe that a single FAI is the best strategy?

With the amount of information that can be sent down a wire increasing
even faster than Moore's law it will be pointless to talk about two
separate AI’s unless they were at such a huge distance apart that the
delay caused by speed of light became significant. Everything one knew
(or felt) the other would too.
