Newton's Wake: A Space Opera
*
By Ken MacLeod
*
Tor Books
*
Hardcover, June 2004
*
320 pages
*
ISBN: 0-765-30503-8

During a war in the 21st century between the United States and Europe, the
war machine artificial intelligences underwent a rapid evolution, sparking
a singularity event, the Hard Rapture, which resulted in most of humanity
dead and/or scanned and stored by the AIs. Most of the AIs left Earth to
continue their evolution, and the remnants of humanity that did not flee
were left struggling to survive. [etc]
