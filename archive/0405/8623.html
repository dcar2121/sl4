Nobody likes to be bored but there could be dangers in changing the point
where it kicks in if you didn’t know what you were doing. Set it too high
and:

“I love the way this little red rubber ball bounces up and down, weee
look at it go! One, two, three, …., 99422, 99423…”

Set it too low and:

“I don’t want to learn how to pack my parachute properly, it’s boring.”

John K Clark
