Spike Jones will host the first event of a new quarterly gathering of
Bay Area transhumanists (what shall we name ourselves?) on Sunday, March
6th 2005 at 5PM, in his house at 904 La Palma, Milpitas, CA, near the
intersection of I-680 and Jacklin Road. The nominal theme of this
gathering is "Eliezer's Welcome Party" and I hope to meet my many new
neighbors. To ease the burden on our gracious host, the dinner is
potluck - bring as much food as you'd like to eat yourself, and we'll share.

Interested in hosting a future gathering? Talk to Spike or myself.
