We are pleased to announce the 2004 $10,000 Challenge Grant for
the continued research of Research Fellow Eliezer Yudkowsky. All
contributions toward the Challenge Grant will be matched dollar for
dollar. If we meet the entire Challenge, the resulting $20,000 will
sponsor Yudkowsky's full-time research on Friendly AI theory until
at least November 2005. The Challenge will run until October 9th.
