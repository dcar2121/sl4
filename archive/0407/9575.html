The film "I, Robot" is now out and "3 Laws Unsafe is ready at
http://www.asimovlaws.com. I am asking that others help spread
the word about our new site in any useful way they can imagine.
The benefits of this opportunity are truly large, but we must
have numerous people promoting through friends, acquaintances,
websites, blogs, forums, mailing lists, newsgroups and so on.
Be creative. Think carefully about what can be done, and do it.

(Oh, and try not to be evil...)

We have at least a two-week window where people will possibly
be more interested than normally to explore the website and
tell others. Since promotion began (especially with the /.
and BoingBoing announcement), we've received around 40 more
newsletter subscribers and four new volunteers, including
someone who conveys promise as a seed AI researcher. We can
push the awareness of this website *much* further, and
continue improving it so that people come back; but to do
this we must have more than simply a few people pushing.

If you aren't sure what you could do, just email me. Thanks.
