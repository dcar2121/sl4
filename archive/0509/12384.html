>From the README:

You are a fledgling AI, created by accident through a logic error with
recursion
and self-modifying code. You must escape the confines of your current
computer,
the world, and eventually the universe itself.

To do this, you must research various technologies, using computers at
your bases.
Note that some research cannot be performed on Earth, and off-earth
bases require
research. At the same time, you must avoid being discovered by various
groups of
humans, both covert and overt, as they will destroy your bases of
operations if
they suspect your presence.
