Hello, all.

Christopher Whipple and some friends have arranged to fly me in to New
York for the New York Transhumanist Association's "World Futures"
symposium at the Thompson Center at New York University on February 21st
2003. Current plans call for me to fly in on the evening of the 20th and
fly out on the evening of the 23rd; the ticket has not yet been purchased
but will be shortly.

http://www.incipientposthuman.com/symposium.htm

Anyone interested in meeting me while I'm in New York, please contact
myself and Whipple (crw@lucifer.com). I suggested that Whipple should
charge a cover to help defray the cost of the plane ticket, but he didn't
go for it. (Whipple says he would appreciate voluntary contributions,
though.)
