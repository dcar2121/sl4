Of possible interest to those list members not yet aware of it, the
site http://www.orionsarm.com/ features speculative fiction and
pseudo-non-fiction (timelines, encyclopedia entries, etc.) at SL3-4
levels, brought together in a sort of "universe-building scifi
backstory" way. Nicely done site (despite the cheesy entrance
graphic/blurb).

(I'd recommend the http://www.orionsarm.com/topics.html page or
the Encyclopedia Galactica link over the Background link as an
entrypoint.)
