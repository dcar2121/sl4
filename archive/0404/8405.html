Welcome!

I have joined the race less than two years ago, with a
more modest goal, of creating a "smarter than any human
programmer".

We have already succeed with Critticall, which has some
easily verifiable results, such as "Several Unique
Sort", faster than any other known sort for a
particular case, where there is not many unique records
- only a lot of records!

Or an improved Dijkstra's algorithm for the next
lexical permutation and some others also.

The case is well documented on the dedicated web site:
http://www.critticall.com
