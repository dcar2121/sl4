My only comment is that the time constant seems to
be closer to one year currently than to 18 months.
I just ordered Saturday a new PC (Athlon XP-1800+),
which in performance is almost double my previous
machine (900 MHz Athlon). The previous machine was
bought a year ago Nov 15th, and cost 7% more in
constant dollars, so it's pretty close to a doubling
in performance/cost in one year.
