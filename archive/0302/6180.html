I'll actually begin with a short introduction, as I'm new to the list. My name is Tyrone Pow, I live in Canada, and I recently volunteered my efforts as a professional website and graphic designer to help The Singularity Institute establish it's 'corporate identity'.
 
In my efforts to create an appropriate and inspiring logo that captures the essence and spirit of the Singularitarian movement, there were a few rejected designs. Determined to not let my efforts go /entirely/ unappreciated, I thought I'd share the various designs submitted thus far. None of these were met with satisfaction.
 
http://www.tyronepow.com/raven/singinst-rejectedlogos.gif
 
Stay tuned for the new logo to rear it's beautiful head in the coming weeks.
