Yes, I can recommend several you would probably enjoy. Cory Doctorow's
Down and Out In The Magic Kingdom is a free download:
http://craphound.com/down/ Cory's short story 0wnz0red is available on
Salon.com: http://www.salon.com/tech/feature/2002/08/28/0wnz0red/
Greg Egan has several free short stories on line, including
Border Guards: http://gregegan.customer.netspace.net.au/BORDER/Complete/Border.html
Oceanic: http://gregegan.customer.netspace.net.au/OCEANIC/Complete/Oceanic.html
The Planck Dive:
http://gregegan.customer.netspace.net.au/PLANCK/Complete/Planck.html

The following are not free downloads but are excellent examples of a
crunchy technological coating with a chewy philosophical center.
Diaspora, by Greg Egan.
Permutation City, by Greg Egan.
Schismatrix Plus, by Bruce Sterling.
Steel Beach, by John Varley.
Transmetropolitan, by Warren Ellis.
Fossil Games, by Tom Purdom.
I hope you enjoy them.
-Matt
