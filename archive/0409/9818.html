After Accelerating Change 2004, come to this Wednesday's chat to
discuss anything interesting that happened there. You are especially
encouraged to come if you attended to the conference and share your
knowledge with the community. The chat starts at 9 PM Eastern Time and
will run for about two hours.

To participate through a standard IRC client, connect to "sl4.org" on a
standard IRC port (for example, 6667) and join channel #sl4:
          irc://sl4.org/sl4

To participate through Java applet, point your browser at
    http://sl4.org/chat/

To clear up any confusion about timezones, go to
    http://www.time.gov/timezone.cgi?Eastern/d/-5/java
to see the current time in Atlanta, GA.

To see the calendar for this and other SL4 events, visit
    
http://ical.mac.com/WebObjects/iCal.woa/wa/default?u=redbird&n=SL4.ics
