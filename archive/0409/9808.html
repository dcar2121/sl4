Well, the article text makes it clear that it's some kind of projection on
a reflective piece of cloth, as shown in the (almost illegible) black and
with diagram before the movies. To replicate a moving background one would
need a camera with a FOV-adjusted view. Not easy, but doable. The shortcut
that makes it possible is that the image is projected on the clothes, not
emitted by them. Of course it wiil also work in only one direction.

Alfio
