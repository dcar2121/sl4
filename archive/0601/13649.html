Hi all,

The purpose of this e-mail is to announce AGIRI's 2006 "AGI Workshop",
which is described at:

http://www.agiri.org/forum/index.php?showtopic=23

This is intended as a limited-attendance workshop: we want to keep it
small so as to ensure a high quality of discussion and an
intimate/intense atmosphere. Attendance is limited to those who are
actively involved in AGI or closely-AGI-related research, or else
actively involved in philosophical, public-service or business
pursuits related to AGI. If you meet this description and are
interested in attending please email me with a brief overview of your
background and a description of your interest in and relationship to
AGI.

We are also seeking a limited number of additional presenters for the
workshop section focusing on "Practical AGI Projects." If you are an
active participant in an AI project directly aimed at creating AGI and
would like to give a brief presentation at the workshop, please let me
know.

Thanks
Ben Goertzel
