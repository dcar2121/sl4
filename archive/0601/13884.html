Hi,

I've found a URL that might be of interest to some of you. It's about
automated debugging, basically you can submit a linux-i386 binary to the
system, with a fail and correct run, and it will build a causal model on why
the failure happened. While I don't think it's likely to put developers, or
debuggers, out of their jobs, it appears to be a seriously interesting
technology insofar as reducing software programming and debugging complexity
is concerned.

The URL of the FAQ: http://www.st.cs.uni-sb.de/askigor/faq.php
