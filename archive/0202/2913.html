I don't want to see this discussion cut off entirely. However, because of
past experience with this issue, I have decided to promulgate the
following *new* rule: There is a one-a-day limit on posts arguing about
objective versus subjective morality, whether "rightness" can be defined,
and so on.

That one post per day may be any length, and may contain replies to any
number of other posters, but please limit it to one per day.

The discussion will hopefully still take place. Just over a longer period
of time and with longer posts.

This rule goes into effect as soon as you read this message.
