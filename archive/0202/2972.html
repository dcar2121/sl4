Arona Ndiaye wrote:
>
> Talking about social life, if my other half wishes to log in, is that
> allowed ?

In the biweekly, unofficial ones, yes. The monthly ones should probably be
saved for advanced topics. Bringing your SO to that, if your SO isn't already
up on transhumanism and such, isn't nice. In the words of Nikolai Kingsley,
it probably feels like playing a dungeon game and suddenly being dropped down
to level twenty-three when you're a level two fighter.
