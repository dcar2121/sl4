At the suggestion of Michael Raimondi, SL4 will hold weekly "unofficial"
chats. The rules for these will be different from the monthly chats; I
won't be actively moderating, and beginners are explicitly allowed to show
up and hopefully get better answers than Sarah did.

People are still showing up in the SL4 chat room every now and then, and
there might as well be an explicit rendezvous time.

Time: Saturday of each week, 8PM EST, 5PM PST.

The next monthly chat is still scheduled for Wednesday, March 6th, at 8PM
EST.

To get to the chat, use the IRC client of your choice to visit the server
extropy.org:6667 and join channel #sl4. Or use Extropy's chat clients at
http://www.extropy.org/bbs/ and type /join #sl4. To see current EST,
visit http://www.time.gov/timezone.cgi?Eastern/d/-5/java
