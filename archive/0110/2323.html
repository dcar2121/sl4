The logo should be inspired by the concept of rhizome. It would be more
representative than a tree since a sysop is all about multiplicity and
should not be visually reduced as a centralized hierarchy. Think about the
net, or a plant of potatoes. The capacity of evolution should be included,
think of the mutation of the cell of a cat when connected by air with the
genetic material of a baboon. I propose the face of a cat with the face of a
baboon on its lower lip, the background is a web of fern roots.
