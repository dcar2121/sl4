There are still limits inherent in the physical constants of the universe. No unicorns, for instance. No faster than light velocities. No quantum system beyond a dust-mote in diameter. The first technical limit we may exploit in our technology is quantum encryption. It is our first weapon against an AGI takeover. An AGI cannot jam a quantumly-encrypted message without being detected. We will defeat the robots in the inevitable death-match!!!!!!!!!!!!
