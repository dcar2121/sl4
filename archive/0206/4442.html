> On 27.06.2002 6:02 Uhr, "Stephen Reed" <reed@cyc.com> wrote:
>
> > With a super-peer network, we should have over 10,000 OpenCyc servers
> > under central/hierarchical control for running Cyc applications.
>
> So it's decided now. Cyc will be the seed...

Of course I wish to position OpenCyc down the path to becoming a Seed AI,
but Cyc is very far from having that capability and having more Cycs work
together is actually not much of an advancement - until such time as Cyc
can improve itself.

However the existence of a broad knowledge-exchange network would continue
to attract developers to the open source OpenCyc project.
