Welcome, Lee.

I too saw the Singularity long ago, circa 1982 (I didn't have a name for
it), but after serious and extended thought on the subject, stepped away
from its study because I found the computing power then available to me, to
be pitifully inadequate. In 1998 I reassessed the situation, and found that
computing power would soon be catching up with my imagination... and so that
is why I am on SL4, and studying and working hard to 'bring it on'.

If you haven't read the SL4 archives, I recommend them to you as a
primo-excellent resource.
