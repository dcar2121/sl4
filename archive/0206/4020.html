Michael Warnock wrote:

>
> What are your pet data structures?
> Do you think its more valuable for creating AGI to have
> the perfect programming environment or the most well
> understood/used/available one?
>

Pet data structures? Not really... whatever works in a given situation.

Perfect programming environment? It'll never happen... but the one to
choose for AGI should have both wide availability and good tools.

Availability: so that you can find good people to 'plug in' to you team
without long ramp-up times.

Good tools: so that the team can be efficient.
