As we all know, books are not information. Books are pictures of
information. Real information is information you can copy, paste, and
search.

Does anyone know of any web pages on rationality - the online equivalents of
"Zen and the Art of Motorcycle Maintenance", say - that they would highly
recommend? I'm not asking for a list of the first 10 pages returned by
Googling on "rationality"; I can do that too... however, if you find a
really good page on rationality through Googling, feel free to forward it
on.
