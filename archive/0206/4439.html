I'm unable to participate properly (due the the accursed dayjob), but I
would like to remark that I haven't really expected self-regulation to
work here. It is an extremely self-selected group.

acknowledged, given that the fundamental risk assessment issue (the
showstopper) is being completely ignored.

suggested regulations for the field, in the hope someone will find that
useful.

I largely put the hope on the task guarding itself due to its intrinsic
unaccessibility, though. I'd like to encourage you to post results,
especially if there are hints at criticalilty.
