Short term goals verses long term goals.

Whether a goal is short term or long is not the determining factor of precedence.

Most things of great value require great effort to achieve, therefore, *MOST* long term goals are more important than short term. But the term length or effort is not the determining factors of precedence. The value of the goal is.
