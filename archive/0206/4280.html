Eliezer S. Yudkowsky wrote:

 I am uncertain as you, or more so,
but
my uncertainty is a volume centered around the positive feedback loop
that
seems less odd for an AI than the strange human way of doing things, not
a
volume centered around the human world.

### That's all I (and some others wanted to hear). It *is* a feature (not a
bug) of sentients to doubt. As it happens, the centerpoint of my own
superposition of hunches is close to yours.

Rafal
