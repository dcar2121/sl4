> everything from gunpowder

In the days of the Roman Empire, you would send
thousands of legionnaires to a city in order to
destroy it. Nowadays, defense contractors hire
thousands of employees to build the aircraft and
bombs. A smaller number of soldiers deliver the
weapons to destroy a city today, but the overall
productivity in destroying cities hasn't improved
as much as you might think.
