> Ben claims that Powers will be indifferent to humanity.

No, I did not and do not claim this.

I merely presented this as my best intuitive guess. I attach a fairly low
confidence to this guess of mine.

One of my reasons for putting forth this guess is because it seems to me
this possibility is generally ignored.

Instead, people tend to focus on various options in which Powers are closely
engaged with humans: either helping us as our Gods, or seeking to
exterminate us.

I don't know what's gonna happen, and neither does any other human....

-- Ben G
