TransVision 2004 was held 6-8 August. This Wednesday (today, 11
August), join us in #sl4 to discuss the conference from 9 to 11 PM ET.
We especially encourage your participation if you attended TransVision.
  Like our Post Foresight chat, this is a chance for everyone who didn't
go to find out what happened (and if they want to go in the future) and
for attendees to share their experiences with the community.

For more information about TransVision 2004, visit
    http://www.transhumanism.org/tv/2004/

To participate through a standard IRC client, connect to "sl4.org" on a
standard IRC port (for example, 6667) and join channel #sl4:
    irc://sl4.org/sl4

To participate through Java applet, point your browser at
    http://sl4.org/chat/

To clear up any confusion about timezones, go to
    http://www.time.gov/timezone.cgi?Eastern/d/-5/java
to see the current time in Atlanta, GA.

To see the calendar for this and other SL4 events, visit
    
http://ical.mac.com/WebObjects/iCal.woa/wa/default?u=redbird&n=SL4.ics
