some physical brain's exercises, therefore some physical processes
inside of *this* Universe. Mathematics is all inside neurology. At
least for now. But will remain forever inside some Turing machine.

If this final frame is the physics or the mathematics, is a semantic
question. But even finite as it is, it's huge.
