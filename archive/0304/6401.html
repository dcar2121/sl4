 --- Mike Williams <mikew12345@lvcm.com> wrote: > I'm
> If there's no
> potential for any
> interaction between 2 universes, then any discussion
> of universes beyond our
> own is moot.

This i would call reason blindness. The idea that
because you cannot interact with something physically
it means you cannot use any reasoning to work out an
ontology for these things that cannot be interacted
with. Needless to say this is a very narrow and
limiting view. It means that out of all things which
exist you can only play around with the infinitesimal
number of universes which are capable of interacting
with each other (such as the QM multiverse) and your
mind is not allowed to explore the generic structure
of the metaverse as a whole.
