Rebecca wrote:
> While we're solving Great Problems In Definitions, how many grains of
> sand makes a heap?

I put that one to Marcello - "If you keep removing grains of sand from
a heap, when does it stop being a heap?"

Marcello hadn't heard it before, but he still answered - *instantly* -
"Never, when all the sand is gone, it's a heap of zero grains."

Now that's a mathematical mind.
