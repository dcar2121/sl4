Eliezer wrote:
> Incidentally, if at first you don't succeed - give up!

I don't quite agree with this. The effects of the Singularity will affect these people too -- shouldn't we put some effort into at least trying to make them understand that it could be a good thing? Won't the Singularity have rather negative effects on a race that is more or less unprepared? Although it is a cliche -- let's face it, those high school kids are the future!
