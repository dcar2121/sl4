> What is interesting about a "super AI" is

this super AI will do next and outsmart it. In other words there will be
enormous pressure to develop a Hyper AI. And then there will be enormous
pressure to outsmart the Hyper AI so….

Even the Singularity will not invalidate the laws of Evolution, even if
it now works on Memes not Genes.
