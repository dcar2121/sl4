infrastructure for collaborative computing is building even more
rapidly than I originally thought, so when the actual software
becomes available, the change will be precipitous.

network, and an enhanced ability to take over that computing power. This
means that anyone who has a use for it and doesn't mind breaking the
rules can control a million computers. All we need is software.
