We'd like to make the SIAI symbol an AI hand (ones and zeroes) shaking
hands with a human hand. Unfortunately we don't have anyone here who can
produce this as a professional-looking logo. What we need is one
simplified black-and-white logo for envelopes and so on, a la the classic
Foresight "molecular gear", and one realistic color image. Maybe also a
third image, at an intermediate level of detail, for the website. If
nobody can do a professional-looking logo on a volunteer basis, we may
have to actually pay to get it done.
