9. Blessed are they that defy both criticism and praise.

Thanks. But seriously, I don't think that ideas - *especially* good ideas
- should be attached to people. Good ideas are independent of the people
who propose them; at least, good ideas of the nontechnical sort.

On a totally unrelated note, I prefer to go by "Eliezer" in text. "Eli"
is only for speech. In writing it looks like a brand of cheesecake. Just
one of my little quirks...
