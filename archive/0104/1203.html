You too can now have a bound, printed version of "Friendly AI", as seen at
the Foresight Gathering. Just print the page above, take the printout to
Kinko's, and ask for a wire binding. (No, there is not, as yet, any way
to order a copy from the Singularity Institute.) Note that the printable
version of CaTAI includes the printable version of FAI.
