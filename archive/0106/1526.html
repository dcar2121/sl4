>out of this here box, I'm going to put all 11 rounds straight through your CPU,

threaten to kill Ver? Sounds like murder to me, or at least justifiable homicide. (and
I know homicide is the wrong word to use, I'll happily entertain substitutes) I
suppose that you could argue it as a case of self defense, although entering the box
with a gun, and threatening harm, might be seen as premeditation. If you want to act
in accordance with the same rules of Friendliness as your eventual Sysop, what exactly
do you do in this instance? Sorry if this has been discussed before.
