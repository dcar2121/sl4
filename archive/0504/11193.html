Eliezer S. Yudkowsky wrote:

> as far as I can tell any actual
> intelligence needs both the decision
> and the prediction component,

Predicting how much pain, pleasure or some other arbitrary measure, a possible action will bring, and selecting the best action by its _predicted_ value - that's the decision mechanism.
