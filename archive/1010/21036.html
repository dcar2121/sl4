The meeting will take place on Friday, November 5, at 10.00, in Bar Ilan
University, Katz building (also known as Moreshet Israel 604 building, in
the "Northern" part of the campus, in the "asbestonim" area), third floor,
"Seminars Room" (room 309).

The meeting will be dedicated to the subject of singularity and artificial
intelligence.

In the program:

10.00 – 11.00 - Anna Salamon (Singularity Institute for Artificial
Intelligence, US) - Transhuman rationality

11.00 – 12.00 – Joshua Fox – Human Intelligence, Artificial Intelligence

12.00 – 13.00 – Sergio Stein – The Basics of Technological Singularity.

The meeting is open and attendance is free.

Please contact Ilia Stambler <ilia.stambler@gmail.com> with questions.

Facebook Event: http://www.facebook.com/event.php?eid=160599100626811
