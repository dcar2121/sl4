 Today, Humanity+ officially opens registration for its next
conference, Humanity+
@ Caltech<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Humanity/51bdd805fc/89bb07d154/c2ceebbb2e>:
Redefining Humanity in the Era of Radical Technological Change. Humanity+ @
Caltech <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Humanity/51bdd805fc/89bb07d154/65a3b1c85f>will
take place on December 4-5 (Saturday/Sunday), at the Beckman
Institute<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Humanity/51bdd805fc/89bb07d154/5d9c4a89e8>at
Caltech in Los Angeles, California.
Speakers <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Humanity/51bdd805fc/89bb07d154/62c4b29fe5>will
include many of the top visionaries and leaders of the transhumanist
community, as well as new voices from the worlds of science, art, media and
business.

The Humanity+ @ Caltech
program<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Humanity/51bdd805fc/89bb07d154/29767b984d>will
be divided into four main sessions, each one of which will cover a key
area of transhumanist thought:

    * Re-Imagining Humans: Mind, Media and Methods (Saturday morning)
    * Radically Increasing the Human Healthspan (Saturday afternoon)
    * Redefining Intelligence: Artificial Intelligence, Intelligence
Enhancement and Substrate-Independent Minds (Sunday morning)
    * Business and Economy in the Era of Radical Technomorphosis (Sunday
afternoon)

Tickets for the
conference<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Humanity/51bdd805fc/89bb07d154/89416a1bc8>will
be $299 per person ($179 for students). If you register
before November
1st<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Humanity/51bdd805fc/89bb07d154/e61e88b17b>,
you will be eligible for a discounted, Early Registration price of $249
($149 for students), so register today! There will also be a special
banquet<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Humanity/51bdd805fc/89bb07d154/5cfa24b22c>for
conference attendees after the end of the first day, on Saturday
evening. Tickets for the
banquet<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Humanity/51bdd805fc/89bb07d154/fd9dcb8407>are
$39; space is limited, so register soon to ensure your seat.
(Breakfast
and lunch are included free for both days.)

Humanity+ conferences are open to everyone, but there is a discount for
members <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Humanity/51bdd805fc/89bb07d154/997eadb78e>.
If you are a full member of Humanity+, you can get a 10% discount on
conference registration, as well as discounts on future conferences.
Humanity+ is also launching a new membership program, Plus
membership<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Humanity/51bdd805fc/89bb07d154/f696128f5c>,
for transhumanist enthusiasts. Plus members will get a 50% discount on
Humanity+ @ Caltech registration, and free or heavily discounted tickets to
future conferences. We also expect to announce additional benefits for Plus
members over the next year.

If you are already a member of Humanity+, the discount code for conference
registration should be emailed to you shortly. If you're not a member of
Humanity+, it's not too late to join! Follow this link to join Humanity+
before November
1st<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Humanity/51bdd805fc/89bb07d154/9f9ddf6b82>,
and you can receive all of the benefits of membership, including conference
discounts.

If you are too far away or otherwise unable to make it to L.A., don't worry!
Humanity+ has chapters<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Humanity/51bdd805fc/89bb07d154/2b4538a699>all
over the world, and many of them hold free
local meetups<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Humanity/51bdd805fc/89bb07d154/c337ba8360>for
transhumanists in their area. We've also set up a Twitter
feed <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Humanity/51bdd805fc/89bb07d154/953c6ad0fa>, so
that you can be informed when a Humanity+ meetup is happening near you. If
you or your friends are holding a meetup, just email us at
info@humanityplus.org to let us know, and we'll help you get the word out.

Humanity+ @ CalTech is hosted by the California Institute of
Technology<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Humanity/51bdd805fc/89bb07d154/1186dfb93f>and
ab|inventio<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Humanity/51bdd805fc/89bb07d154/2bca26f6f9>,
the invention factory behind QLess, Whozat, SocialDiligence and MyNew.TV.
Sponsorship is also provided by
TechZulu<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Humanity/51bdd805fc/89bb07d154/88db3b827c>and
Vokle <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Humanity/51bdd805fc/89bb07d154/071b900da2>.
