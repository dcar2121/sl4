Personally I'm trying to read MITECS cover-to-cover to make sure I don't
miss anything (I still haven't finished this, since I don't get around to it
very often nowadays). But I find that a fun way to read MITECS is to simply
open the cover. If that doesn't work, open the cover again. I've yet to
see both tries fail to find something interesting.
