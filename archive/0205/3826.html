It has nothing to do with AI unfortunately. It is just a large and
complicated engineering and human factors project. We are (I was)
developing the main integrated 3D battlespace visualization where as
CMU/MayaViz is developing the main integrated 2D battlespace
visualization. Unfortunately I can not discuss or disclose much on this
public list except to point you to the very small (and horrible)
screenshot of it on my webpage:
  http://www.exocortex.org/ben/programming.html

During the 2 summers I worked on this project I contributed or rewrote a
majority of the code that underlies the visualization -- about 1.2MB of
a total 1.7MB of java code.
