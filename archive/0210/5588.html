The Singularity Institute is supporting a new mailing list:
volunteers@intelligence.org

Volunteers for Singularity projects use this list to coordinate their work and
chat among themselves. List membership is open; new members may join
automatically and post without moderation. However, this list is for active
volunteers only. For subscription information, see
http://intelligence.org/lists/volunteers.html

There is also a new IRC chatroom, #siaiv at intelligence.org:6667
