The Singularity Institute <http://intelligence.org/> has moved to a new ISP,
and a large amount of new content has just gone live, including:

"What is the Singularity?"
"Why Work Toward the Singularity"
(The above two pages replace the now-outdated "An Introduction to the
Singularity", which has been taken offline.)
"Singularity Institute FAQ"
"Why Small Donations Matter"
An "Introductions" section including 12 short articles on the Singularity,
Artificial Intelligence, Friendly AI, seed AI, the Singularity's impact,
activism, the long-term Singularity effort, AI as a Singularity strategy,
AI theory, nanotechnology, and uploading.

See http://intelligence.org/news/ for links to the above pages.
