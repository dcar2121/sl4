is a monster and is chasing me with a bloody ax is due to his genetics,

quantum variation in the space time continuum. I really don’t give a
damn. For whatever reason or for no reason at all he’s still s monster
and I want him dead. End of story.

free will. At the end of the day everything, absolutely positively 100%
everything, is due to cause and effect OR it is not.
