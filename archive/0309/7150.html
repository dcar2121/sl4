There will be a special guest chat with Nick Bostrom on Thursday, 18
September, 2003 at 7 PM ET.

(Officially the chat commences at 0:01 Friday GMT+1, Nick's time.)

Come chat with Nick Bostrom, PhD, Oxford University, Research Fellow,
regarding his transhumanist and Singularity relevant writing.

http://www.nickbostrom.com/

To participate through a standard IRC client, connect to "sl4.org" on a
standard IRC port (for example, 6667) and join channel #sl4:
        irc://sl4.org/sl4

To participate through Java applet, point your browser at:
        http://sl4.org/chat/

To clear up any confusion about timezones, go to
        http://www.time.gov/timezone.cgi?Eastern/d/-5/java
to see the current time in Atlanta, GA.
