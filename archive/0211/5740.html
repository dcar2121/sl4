Reason wrote:

> Unless some people have written good stuff in the past few years, building
> custom graphs on the fly is a horribly expensive development project (in
> comparison to the rest of it), just FYI.

It depends on your tools. The .NET framework has a very nice facility for
doing this - you just instantiate a bitmap object, draw on it as if it were
a desktop UI window, and then call a method to get a byte array in whatever
file format you want. It can take a bit of work for a complex image, but it
isn't hard to do.
