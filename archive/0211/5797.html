What is so very difficult about a notion of freedom that amounts
to one being free to do whatever one wishes (when the contraints
of physics, limited time, resources and so on) that does not
forceably interfere with the same freedom of others? I don't
see why various subjective qualities are actually relevant to
freedom in the political/economic sense. Nor do I see that an
FAI need pose any danger to this freedom.
