> Morals are not weak or strong, only different. Whatever happens to the

Weak morals weaken and strong morals strengthen societies. Postmodern
notions of 'everything is relative' are sounding increasingly hollow.
Objectivity when making value judgments are not only possible but necessary
in virtually every decision one makes.

The moral framework at play when answering the question "What shall I/we
do?" is the decisive factor determining the fate from the individual all the
way to nation states.
