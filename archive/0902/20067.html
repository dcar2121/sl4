STAND-UP is the collaborative effort of One World Association and Poznan
Academy of Fine Arts which focuses on human rights issue by using film,
narrative, interactive media, and digital arts as a medium. This project
responds to the growing needs for acceptance and respect to diversity,
dialog and social reflection. http://hrart.wordpress.com/
