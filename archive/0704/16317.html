Decade of the Mind Symposium
May 21, 2007 - 9am - 5pm
May 22, 2007 - 8:30am - 1:30pm
Johnson Center Cinema
GMU Fairfax Campus
Fairfax, VA, USA

Speakers
George Bekey • John Holland • Nancy Kanwisher
Dharmendra Modha • Gordon Shepherd
Vernon Smith • Giulio Tononi

The Krasnow Institute for Advanced Study announces leading
researchers on many varied disciplines of brain study, including
neuroscience, neurobiology, computer science, psychology, robotics
and economics. These speakers will converge at George Mason
University to launch what is anticipated to be a decade long
intensive research initiative into the MIND.

Free (registration required)
For the most current symposium information visit:
krasnow.gmu.edu/decade
or contact: Krasnow Institute for Advanced Study
