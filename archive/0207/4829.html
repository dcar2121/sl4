Anand wrote:
>
> A lot of us have dreams but many do not know how to effectively achieve
> them. One of my dreams has been to _effectively_ help people (e.g., by
> improving their choices in life). Learning how to effectively help people
> is one problem and the main determinant of our ability to solve it is our
> quality of intelligence. Therefore, solving the problem of effectively and
> safely improving our quality of intelligence is the most effective way to
> solve all remaining problems.

Sorry. The last sentence should read, "Therefore, solving the problem of
effectively and safely improving our quality of intelligence is the most
effective way to help people and also solve all remaining problems."
