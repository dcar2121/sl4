Most mailing lists have a little footer at the bottom of their messages
describing mailing list management information, such as how to unsubscribe
and change subscription options. The ones that don't, have the URL to this
information, or send out an email filled with such information every month
or so. I recommend that this mailing list have some list management
mechanism.
