> Anyone have any additional answers to add?

> Why do we as humans, feel the need to create something with the possibility
> of being much greater than us?

 I call them "kids".

> interms of Intelligence and awareness (And even possibly, in Physical
> Strength.)

 I want them to be better than me in all ways, including health, education,
intelligence, and financial standing. I want them to have a better environment
that I have, to foster those things and to keep the maximum open possibilities.

> create something with the possibility of being much greater than us?

 Because one day I'll be dead and gone.
