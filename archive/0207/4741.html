A 50/50 chance. Yes, those are your odds. And yes it could do things vastly worse or better than my examples. And you may be able to catch a poorly formed AI. But a sane intellectually superior AI of either variety can tell you all the things that would make you feel better and you would be no closer to knowing. So your odds are 50/50. You can't improve them by waiting. You can only lower your overall odds of survival by waiting. So place your bets, take your chances, and pull the lever.

Mike.
