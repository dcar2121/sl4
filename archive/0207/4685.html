On Thu, 04 July 2002, James Higgins wrote:

> Please don't pretend to know what
> a Singularity would do.

I don't. I assume, it would be super rational, only. Otherwise, it's a failed Singularity. Not a friendly, at least.

I see the total unpredictability of a Singularity as a myth.

It's not (only) the question of the resources - it's of the morality. Some Powers wouldn't permit AIDS in Africa, if they were not morally defected.

I can't know for sure, of course, but any "ZOO" - is highly unlikely to me.
