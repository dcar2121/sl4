Gordon Worley wrote:

> Q: So, does intelligence equal wisdom?
>
> A: It has become cliché on SL4 to say that intelligence does not

> time. Please, avoid pointing this out unless the alternative is being
> drawn and quartered (and maybe not even then). This is also a rather

> extrapolation).
