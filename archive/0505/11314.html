data types is posted on the SL4 wiki:

"The following schematic proposes 28 universal data
types for sentient minds. It is proposed that 7
universal knowledge domains (Matter, Mathematics,
Mentality, Meaning, Models, Morality and Mind) and 4
universal mental operations (Action plans, Deductions,
Inductions and Representations) are sufficient for a
true general intelligence (one capable of universal
reasoning, or reasoning in all domains in which a
sentient mind could logically exist)."
