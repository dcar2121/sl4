Last March, the Singularity Institute launched a "Challenge Grant
Challenge", seeking major donors to pledge a $5,000 Challenge Grant for
Eliezer Yudkowsky's Research Fellowship. This effort has succeeded and
we are now announcing the Fellowship Challenge Grant.

The Fellowship Challenge will run for 90 days, until September 12, 2003.
Any donations designated for the Fellowship Challenge will be matched
dollar-for-dollar by pledgers, up to total donations of $5,000. If the
Fellowship Challenge is fully achieved, the resulting combined amount of
$10,000 will be enough to fund the Research Fellowship through 2003.

For more information, please visit
http://intelligence.org/challenge-grant.html
