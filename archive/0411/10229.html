The $15,000 Fellowship Challenge Grant was successfully filled before
its completion, by a total of 54 donors and $19,786 in donations (owing
to a little last-minute overkill), including all non-subscription Paypal
donations during the Today and Tomorrow campaign. Our public support
percentage is currently in the range of 31%. Our thanks and
congratulations to everyone who donated.
