Emil Gilliam has stepped down from his position as List Sniper, citing lack
of time. Rising to the challenge is Mitch Howe, henceforth to be addressed
as List Sniper Howe. We'll all have to step quietly for a while; the new
List Sniper is bound to be looking for an excuse to wield his arbitrary and
unchallengeable edicts.

The List Sniper has retired: All hail the List Sniper!

Rest assured that despite the deadly power struggles convulsing the inner
circles of SL4, I remain, as ever,

Your unelected tyrant,
Eliezer.
