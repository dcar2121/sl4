Speaking of intuition I thought I'd publically assert
my claims about AGI again (all of which Eli disputes).
 My claims were:

(1) General intelligence without sentience is
impossible.

(2) Total altruism in a general intelligence is
impossible (although say 90% altruism might be
possible)

(3) There is a degree of sentience in everything -
rocks, electrons etc (panpsychism). There will prove
to be a precise scientific way to measure the
sentience of anything.

(4) There is an objective morality.

4 claims put forward by me, 4 claims disputed by
Eliezer. O.K, then, time to put our rival intellects
to the test. Let's wait and eventually the truth or
falsity of all 4 claims should be established. I
really look forward to that day ;)

It's my intellect versus Eliezer's intellect. Who do
you guys bet on?
