Another way to think about it is: hey, let's go ahead and make some progress
towards this thing, but continue learning/questioning along the way. None
of us is saying right now that we are absolutely 100% committed towards
completing an AI. It may turn out years from now that we get it partially
built, and just decide it is too risky based on the data available then.
But let's at least get to that point.
