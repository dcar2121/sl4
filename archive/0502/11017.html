Jeff Medina is our new Volunteer Coordinator. He understands our research
and strategy well, and conveys an ability to produce. He'll be sending an
introduction email soon. You can reach him at analyticphilosophy@gmail.com.

His bio:

http://www.intelligence.org/about.html#medina

Jeff Medina is a philosopher and technology consultant based in the Washington
D.C. metro area. He's a staff member of the Future Technologies Advisory
Group, and an AT&T consultant. In his capacity as a teacher, trainer and
public speaker, he has taught and spoken on topics as diverse as ethics,
physics, human rights, computer science, mechanical engineering, and poetry
at various institutions, including the University of Washington, University
of Toronto, and University of Delaware. As a technologist, Jeff focuses on
object-oriented architecture and design, and has implemented major systems for
IBM, Zurich Financial, and NIIT. He has studied at Harvard, the University
of Delaware, UC Berkeley, SUNY Stony Brook, and the University of London.
