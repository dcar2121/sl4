Very interesting 20 questions game based on AI (they claim) on the
net. I played a few times trying to make it fail and thought of:
myself, a football, several things related to sex, and a soul. The
program performed very well.
20Q.net is an experiment in artificial intelligence. The program is
very simple but its behavior is complex. Everything that it knows and
all questions that it asks were entered by people playing this game.
20Q.net is a learning system; the more it is played, the smarter it
gets.
http://www.20q.net/
