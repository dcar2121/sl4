Welcome to SL4! This list is specifically for discussing *advanced
topics* in futurism, AGI, etc. Please familiarize yourself with some
of the already-published material before posting; it is the explicit
policy of SL4 not to rehash the basics. It's much easier to learn from
reading the literature than browsing the list archives; there are over
10,000 messages in the archives, far too many for any one person to be
familiar with all of them. In particular, see
http://www.intelligence.org/reading/corereading,
http://www.acceleratingfuture.com/tom/bmesa5.pdf,
http://www.yudkowsky.net.

NOTE: By "intelligent systems", we do not mean the types of systems
used in weather forecasting or chess playing. See
http://www.intelligence.org/blog/2007/07/10/the-power-of-intelligence/.
