It has been outdated for years.
Two examples: Hypertonia and Hyperlipidemia, both have been classified
as diseases, but strictly speaking they are not. They are riskfactors
for cardiovascular disease. With the current development of
DNA-analysis and DNA-screening methods more such riskfactors will be
detected and treatments for risk-factors will eventually develop, be
it risk for cancer, dementia, early aging or anything else.
