SL4's monthly chat for December will be Wednesday, 3 December, at 9 PM
Eastern Time.

This month's suggested starter topic:

Singularity Donation

December is the traditional month of giving in the Occident. There's
Christmas, Chanukah, Boxing Day, Festivus, Newtonmas, and, perhaps most
importantly, the End of the Tax Year. With this in mind, what are your
favorite ways of giving to encourage a positive Singularity? Who is
most deserving of our donation dollars and time?

To participate through a standard IRC client, connect to "sl4.org" on a
standard IRC port (for example, 6667) and join channel #sl4:
        irc://sl4.org/sl4

To participate through Java applet, point your browser at:
        http://sl4.org/chat/

To clear up any confusion about timezones, go to
        http://www.time.gov/timezone.cgi?Eastern/d/-5/java
to see the current time in Atlanta, GA.
