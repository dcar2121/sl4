While you're at it, now's the big chance to create nicely formatted
.pdf files of LOGI and CFAI, and to fix the errors in CFAI's "An
Introduction to Goal Systems":

* In "Diagram 1: Simple Choice", the Desirability of Goal C should be
300 rather than 30.
   This way the inherent desirability of World + ~A comes out to (1% *
300 + 99% * 6) = 8.94 as
   the text states.

* In the paragraph following the diagram, "If B's desirability later
changes to 10" should read
   "If B's desirability later changes to 20". This way all the other
numbers are correct.

- Emil
