Theodore Sturgeon wrote _More than Human_, which is very relevant to
your multi-mind/mindplex concept. Simply a fantastic book. It deals
with three people who function together as one organism, and somehow
captures this concept in a way that "feels right". If you do read it,
the second part, which I think is titled _Baby turns Five_, is
sensational.
