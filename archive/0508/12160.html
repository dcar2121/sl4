This explanation of emotion is both clear and insightful. It is probably
worth while for SL4 people to understand emotions well in order to avoid
making errors, and possibly even to increase their number of insights. It
is also valuable in order to help manage one's own motivations and maintain
focus on relevant concerns. However, I hope that it is clear that the way
in which emotions work in humans is only suggestive of one way in which
things could be done in a mind, not the preferred way to do things in an AI.
