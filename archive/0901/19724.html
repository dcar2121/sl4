Hi Matt,

On Mon, Jan 12, 2009 at 3:23 PM, Matt Mahoney <matmahoney@yahoo.com> wrote:
> Simulating past versions of each other does not allow 2 way communication.

Why not?

* You say something (to yourself).
* You simulate the other AI until *it* has simulated *you* enough to
see what you said.
* You simulate it some more, until it says something (to itself) in return.
* You say something in return (to yourself).

Do you see a reason that wouldn't work?
