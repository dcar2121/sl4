With list statistics you have the problem of the ten or twenty regular
posters versus the great unwashed mass of the (mostly) lurkers. The
lurkers are unlikely to take part in polls. (For many and various
good reasons).

So if you just want a poll of the broadcasters (who produce the list)
as opposed to the listeners, then that should be easy and quick.
