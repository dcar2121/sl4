At 11:25 AM 8/6/2006 -0400, Richard Loosemore wrote:

>would anyone be interested in giving me their thoughts about
>*positive* examples of fictional AI?
>
>What I mean is, how many times have SF writers (whether in written
>or movie form) created fictional futures in which the AI was not the villain?

Look at my novel TRANSCENSION for the inevitable ambiguity (this
person is posthuman, after all). The book opens as the AI
speaks/meditates ser love for humankind.

And even more so, the ai of THE WHITE ABACUS.

The situation with the Good Machine in GODPLAYERS and K-MACHINES is
very much more morally complex and ambiguous.
