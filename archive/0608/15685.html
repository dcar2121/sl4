While not as revolutionary as a quantum computer a ballistic computer would
require a new architecture. Very recently a prototype ballistic chip has
been made that operates many thousands of times faster than any conventional
computer chip while producing less heat. Nobody has a precise measurement of
exactly how fast it is because nobody has test equipment that works in the
terahertz range. For more see:

http://arstechnica.com/journals/science.ars/2006/8/17/5022
