It is more or less fine the way it is (modulo the list sniping lapse
that allows SL1 discussions of IQ to ramble on and on... ;-) ).

Politics: kick off people who indulge in pure insults, name-calling,
etc, BUT allow rational, careful discussions of social and political
issues relevant to the singularity or AGI. That much is easy to do.

But the silliest proposal in this discussion is the idea that people
should be banned for unfamiliarity with all of the Singinst website
writings. That site is dominated by Yudkowsky's ramblings, which
represent just one person's opinion.
