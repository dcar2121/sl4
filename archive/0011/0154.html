Biomedical engineer William Ditto is experimenting with "neurocomputers"
consisting of living leech neurons connected to a computer. For further
information, see an article in the October 2000 issue of Discover
(http://www.discover.com/oct_00/feattech.html) and the Web site of the
laboratory where the research is under way
(http://www.physics.gatech.edu/chaos/).
