We were talking about intelligent machines so you might be interested in
a quite good article about the AI program "Watson" designed by IBM to
compete in the quiz show "Jeopardy!"; they intend to have Watson go
against a former Jeopardy human champion and televise the contest
sometime this fall. From our perspective it really doesn't matter if
Watson, or any computer for that matter, is conscious or not; it's
intelligent and getting smarter and we will have to deal with the
consequences.
