  I like your current version of your goal statement - "Friendliness to
sentient beings past, present and future" most out of your versions so far.

  You might want to mention a spacial dimension - eg. "everywhere".

  This goal statement still seems to be some distance away from a goal that

regard for sentient beings (I share it) but lots of people have respect "for

the "Friendliness to sentient beings everywhere, past, present and future"
goal statement?
